---font size---1em vs 1rem---
1em is relative to the 100% of the parent. 2em -> 200% of the parent
1rem is relative not to the parent but to the 100% root, but the highest conventionally what is called the html. 2rem -> 200% of the root.

rem is to be preffered to control varios size on the screen.
